How to sign up

To sign up, you need to head your way over to pekora.zip/auth/application.

Once you have went there, you need to fill out an application. You will also need Discord to create one.

You can choose a referral code, which the next section will be talking about! Once you have filled in your form, you now need to wait for it to be approved.

Referral Codes

Referral codes are user-made codes that users can put in to their application.

When used, the user that used the referral code AND the person that made the referral code will each get 50 robux.

You can make a referral code at pekora.zip/internal/referral.

Robux

Robux is the in-game currency in Korone. You can buy items such as Gamepasses, Developer Products, Shirts, and a lot more with robux.

Tix

Tix is a in-game currency in Korone. You can buy items such as Shirts, T-Shirts, Pants with Tix.

Tix is rarely used.

Builders Club

Builders Club is a Korone Membership that you can get for free. Here's some info about it:

BC = 15 Robux every day.

TBC = 35 Robux every day.

OBC = 85 Robux every day.

Premium = 85 Robux every day.

Head to pekora.zip/internal/membership to get a membership!
